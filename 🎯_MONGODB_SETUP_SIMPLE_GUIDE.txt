STEP 7: UPDATE YOUR PROJECT (30 seconds)
─────────────────────────────────────────

1. Open file: server/.env

2. Find this line:
   MONGODB_URI=mongodb://localhost:27017/fitness-ai

3. Replace with your connection string:
   MONGODB_URI=mongodb+srv://fitai:fitai123@cluster0.xxxxx.mongodb.net/fitness-ai?retryWrites=true&w=majority

   IMPORTANT CHANGES:
   • Replace <password> with: fitai123
   • Replace cluster0.xxxxx with YOUR cluster address
   • Add "/fitness-ai" before the "?"

   Example:
   Before: ...mongodb.net/?retryWrites...
   After:  ...mongodb.net/fitness-ai?retryWrites...

4. Save file (Ctrl+S)

NODE_ENV=development
ML_SERVICE_URL=http://localhost:8000
OPENAI_API_KEY=
GEMINI_API_KEY=

IMPORTANT:
• "cluster0.abc123" will be YOUR cluster address
• "fitai123" is your password
• "/fitness-ai" must be before the "?"
• Keep everything else the same
