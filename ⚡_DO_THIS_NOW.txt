🚀 QUICK FIX (2 MINUTES):

STEP 1: Go to MongoDB Atlas
────────────────────────────
Open: https://www.mongodb.com/cloud/atlas
Click: "Try Free"
Sign up: Use Google/GitHub (fastest)

STEP 2: Create Free Cluster
────────────────────────────
Click: "Build a Database"
Choose: "M0 FREE" tier
Click: "Create Cluster"
Wait: 1-3 minutes

STEP 3: Create User
───────────────────
Click: "Database Access" (left sidebar)
Click: "Add New Database User"
Username: fitai
Password: fitai123
Click: "Add User"

STEP 4: Allow Access
────────────────────
Click: "Network Access" (left sidebar)
Click: "Add IP Address"
Click: "Allow Access from Anywhere"
IP: 0.0.0.0/0
Click: "Confirm"

STEP 5: Get Connection String
──────────────────────────────
Click: "Database" (left sidebar)
Click: "Connect" on your cluster
Choose: "Connect your application"
Copy: The connection string

It looks like:
mongodb+srv://fitai:<password>@cluster0.xxxxx.mongodb.net/?retryWrites=true&w=majority

STEP 6: Update Configuration
─────────────────────────────
Open file: server/.env

Find this line:
MONGODB_URI=mongodb://localhost:27017/fitness-ai

Replace with your connection string:
MONGODB_URI=mongodb+srv://fitai:fitai123@cluster0.xxxxx.mongodb.net/fitness-ai?retryWrites=true&w=majority

(Replace <password> with: fitai123)
(Replace cluster0.xxxxx with your actual cluster address)

Save the file!

STEP 7: Backend Auto-Restarts
──────────────────────────────
✅ Nodemon will detect the change
✅ Backend will restart automatically
✅ You'll see: "MongoDB Connected"

STEP 8: Test Everything
────────────────────────
Open browser: http://localhost:3000
Click: "Get Started Free"
Register: Create your account
✅ DONE! Everything works!
