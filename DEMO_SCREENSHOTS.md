## 🎨 DESIGN FEATURES

### Color Scheme:
- **Primary:** Green (#22c55e) - Health & Growth
- **Secondary:** Blue (#3b82f6) - Trust & Technology
- **Background:** White/Gray (Light) | Dark Gray (Dark Mode)
- **Accents:** Gradients, Shadows, Rounded Corners

### Animations:
- ✨ Fade-in on page load
- ✨ Slide-up for cards
- ✨ Smooth transitions
- ✨ Hover effects
- ✨ Loading spinners

### Typography:
- **Headings:** Bold, Large
- **Body:** Clean, Readable
- **Icons:** Lucide React Icons

### Responsive:
- 📱 Mobile: Single column
- 💻 Tablet: 2 columns
- 🖥️ Desktop: 3-4 columns
